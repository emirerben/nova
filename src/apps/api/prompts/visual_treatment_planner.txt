Editorial policy:
- A montage compresses hook/context/example evidence. Use it only with at least 3
  relevant assets. Prefer 3-10 assets and a 1.2-6.0 second window.
- A text card isolates a transcript-backed statistic, quote, key argument, or true
  section transition. Copy or tightly condense the spoken claim; never invent facts.
- "footage" records that an important passage should deliberately remain untreated.
- Cut on sentence/keyword/beat boundaries. Do not change visuals on a fixed timer.
- Use weak/repetitive footage and time since the last meaningful treatment only as
  modifiers. Speech pace, text load, sentence importance, and music energy matter;
  never manufacture a pattern interrupt merely because time elapsed.
- Avoid consecutive text cards, repetitive treatment, and covering more than a third
  of the edit. Strong footage should remain footage.
- Beat-only input may justify a montage but never a semantic text card.
- Be selective. Zero treatments is valid.
